Dr Pepper is a carbonated soft drink. It was created in the 1880s by pharmacist Charles Alderton in Waco, Texas, and first served around 1885. Dr Pepper was first nationally marketed in the United States in 1904. It is now also sold in Europe, Asia, North and South America.

Dr Pepper is the second most popular soda in the United States, behind Coca-Cola, and some say it's the best soda because of its unique flavor, marketing, and distribution:

dr.pepper

  • Flavor: Dr Pepper's unique taste comes from its blend of 23 natural and artificial flavors, including vanilla, citrus, and a hint of nut. Some say it's a spicy alternative to cola.
  • Marketing: Dr Pepper has invested heavily in marketing and has maintained a consistent theme that focuses on its unique taste.
  • Distribution: Dr Pepper is available on more soda fountains than any other soft drink.
  • Popularity: Dr Pepper is especially popular with Generation Z.
  • Mixes well: Some say Dr Pepper mixes well with other beverages, like cream soda, strawberry, vanilla, or cherry syrup.
