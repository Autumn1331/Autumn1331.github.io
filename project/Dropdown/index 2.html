Chipper the Guniea Pig

Chipper is an American Guinea Pig with a unique fur pattern. Chipper was born in our sanctuary when his mother was surrenderd due to medical issues, sadly she had passed away moments after bring this little guy into the world.

Chipper is a little less than a year old still looking for his forever home. Chipper got his name simply from his sponky, funny, but also loving personality.

Chipper also loves all the attention with adults, to kids, and even all types of animals from dogs, to cats and even birds and reptiles like, iguanas and tortoises.
