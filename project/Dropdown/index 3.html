Tortilla the Sulcata Tortoise

Tortilla is a Sulcata Tortoise also known as the African Spurr Tortoise who is about 7 years old he came to us 2 years ago due to his owner passing away from old age.

Tortilla got his name from his nice tan colors and from his favorite holloween customer that is a flour tortila that he came with that he looks so adorable in.

Tortilla is a very calm and relax tortoise but not when food comes around he might look slow but don't let his laziness fool you he is secertly a racer when he see some good fresh veggies & fruits come his way.
